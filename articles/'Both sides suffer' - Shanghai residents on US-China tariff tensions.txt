Following the 10% tariff by President Trump on all Chinese imports, China has hit back against the US by introducing measures of their own.

China has put a 15% tariff on coal and liquefied natural gas products as well as a 10% tariff on crude oil, agricultural machinery and large-engine cars imported from the US.

Residents of China's largest city, Shanghai, gave their thoughts on the tariff tensions.
